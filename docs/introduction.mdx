## What is this?

The Poker Odds API calculates win equity and tie percentages for 2–10 Texas Hold'em hands. Give it a set of player hands and an optional board, and it returns each player's chances of winning.

## How it works

| Stage | Board cards | Method |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-flop | 0 | Monte Carlo simulation (up to 100,000 iterations) |
| Flop | 3 | Exhaustive enumeration |
| Turn | 4 | Exhaustive enumeration |
| River | 5 | Single deterministic evaluation |

## Card notation

Cards are written as **rank + suit** with no spaces:

| Ranks | Suits |
|---|---|
| `A K Q J T 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2` | `s` (spades) `h` (hearts) `d` (diamonds) `c` (clubs) |

**Examples:** `AsKh` = Ace of Spades + King of Hearts, `2c2d` = Two of Clubs + Two of Diamonds

A hand is two cards concatenated: `AsKh`, `2c2d`, `TsTd`
